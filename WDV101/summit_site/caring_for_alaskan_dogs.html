Caring For Alaskan Dogs

Training

Many alaskan dog breeds are very intelegent and hard working dogs but they must be trained properly. If not trained properly they can develop bad habbits that are hard to break.

Grooming

These dogs should be bathed at least once every 6 weeks and brushed at least once a week because of their thick coat of fur. Before giving them a bath use a hair dryer for a few minutes to blow away any loose hair and lossen up the dirt in their thick fur. You should also make sure to clean their ears and trim their nails and pads and keep their teeth clean.

Health and Nutrition

They need a well balanced diet, vitamins, and healthy treats to keep them healthy and make sure they get plenty of exercise.
