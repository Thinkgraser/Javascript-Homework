About Alaskan Dogs

Alaskan Malamute

The alaskan malmute was orginaly bred for it's stregnth and endurance and were often used as a sled dog. They look similar to wolves because of their pointed ears and long nose. They are great with kids and are very friendly dogs who love attention.

Alaskan Klee Kai

The Alaskan Klee Kai is a small energetic breed that are shy around strangers. They love attention and are easy to train because of their eagerness to please.

Alaskan Husky

The alaskan husky is a very socialable dog. They are bred from siberian huskys and are larger, stronger, and their endurance is better than siberian huskys. They are often used as a racing dog.

Malanees

Malanees are very loving and protective dogs and are great with children. They have thick fur and shed a lot. They can be hard to train.

Alusky

The Alusky is great with children and very loving. They are a working dog and are often used as sled dogs. They are also a good search and rescue dog. They are very playful and energetic. They have a good memory.
