Cocoa Fun Facts

  • It takes a cocoa tree 5 years before it will produce its first cocoa beans
  • The swiss consume 22 pounds of chocolate per capita which is the most in the world
  • Cocoa beans are called cocoa beans instead of cacao because of a mispelling made by english importers in the 18th century
  • Cacao trees are typicaly 13 to 26 feet tall
  • Cocoa flowers will die within 24 hours if they are not pollinated
  • It takes 400 cocoa beans to make a pound of chocolate
  • 300 million tons of cocoa is produced a year
  • It takes 2 to 4 days to make a single serving chocolate bar
