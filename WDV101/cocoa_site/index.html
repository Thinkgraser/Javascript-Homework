Health Benifits of Cocoa

Cocoa contains polyphenols which help reduce inflammation and lower blood presure.

Cocoa also contains flavanols which also help reduce blood pressure.

2 Sweedish studies found the people who eat .7 - 1.1 ounces of cocoa have a reduced chance of heart failure.

A 2 week study found that flavanols in cocoa can increase blood flow to the brain by up to 8% the first week and 10% by the second.
